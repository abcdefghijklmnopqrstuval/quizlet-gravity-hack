Steps (in case you don't know how to use it)
Step 1 : Open the "hack" file above this repo
Step 2 : Copy everything in the file
Step 3 : When you're on the "Start Game" page, open the console (Ctrl+Shift+I and then click "Console").
Step 4 : Paste the code and click enter
Step 5 : Close the console and click "inject"
Step 6 : Choose the score you would like and click "OK"
Step 7 : Click "Start Game" now
Step 8 : You don't even need to play. Once you click "OK" and "Start Game" you are done. 
Step 9 : You can now close the tab if you would like, and see your friends/teachers reactions :)
